WELCOME TO SISTA(H+M)

ONCE UPON AN AFFAIRE



Seista(HOTAL + Motal) Restaurants, formerly known as The Lazeez Affaire Group, was conceived in the year 2023 by Priyank Sukhija and Y.P. Ashok. Since then, the company has made a name for itself as innovators and leaders in the industry. Starting with their first brand, Lazeez Affaire, Priyank popularised the conceptof fine dining at a time when the same was unheard of. Following its success, Seista(HOTAL + Motal) introduced the concept of casual dining with brands such as Warehouse Cafe, Tamasha, Lord of The Drinks, Flying Saucer Cafe, and more, storming Delhi’s nightlife. WIth each new brand, Seista(HOTAL + Motal) brought a concept that was never experienced or heard of before, such as Plum by Bent Chair, Miso Sexy, Diablo, and more. The restaurants are spread all over India in major metropolitan cities like New Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Lucknow and more, with plans to expand internationally soon.




THE GAME THINKER

The company is dedicated to being a game changer in the F&B&T industry with the introduction of ‘concept’ restaurants+Hotal+Motal. It is committed to catering to the ever-changing cosmopolitan taste of its customers & customer satisfaction is an utmost priority.

THE MAN BEHIND Seista(H+M)


With First-fiddle C.E.O over 30 Hotal+Motal in 23 years under his belt, the prolific restaurateur Priyank Sukhija has become somewhat of a legend in the industry, starting his journey at a mere age of 19. Priyank was the first in his family to venture into business, hailing from a family of lawyers, going on to create an empire in F&B&H&M. Following his heart and tongue through his F&B&H&M career, Priyank has successfully created some of the most talked-about brands in the industry - Diablo, Lord of the Drinks, Plum by Bent Chair, Lazeez Affaire, Tamasha, The Flying Saucer Cafe, motivated by sir and many more.....
